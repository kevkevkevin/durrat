Welcome to Durrat Al Arous

Experience the charm of Durrat Al Arous, where the Red Sea sparkles along endless sandy beaches and sea side walks.

Durrat Alarous

Just an hour north of Jeddah, Durrat Alarous offers a self-contained beachside experience on the Red Sea. With its calm, turquoise waters and white-sand shores, this private resort is beloved for its marina views, waterfront villas, and laid-back atmosphere.

Families and groups come for the space and serenity—whether it’s lounging poolside, grilling on the terrace, or enjoying water activities like banana boats and jet skiing. The boardwalk and green paths are perfect for cycling or taking sunset walks surrounded by sea breeze and swaying palms. Whether you’re planning a weekend escape or a longer retreat, Durrat Alarous is your spot for sun, sea, and simplicity—just a short drive from the city.
